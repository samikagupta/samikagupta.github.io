Previous Projects

Influence of Topology and Intervention Methods in Epidemiological Contact Networks (Undergraduate Project)

This project explores different methods to spread infections through various types of networks by making different modifications to network properties and network setups:

  • Different network specific functions and modification capabilities (ER, WS, BA, SBM)
  • Different contagion strategies to spread infection in network (simple spread or spread with recovery)
  • Different vaccination strategies implemented in networks (random, contact tracing, follow link, prioritize)
  • Introducing different methods of developing hesitancy to vaccinations
  • Mutations in network
